The method

The Unlearning Method.

Most coaching adds. More rules, more restriction, more willpower. We do the opposite. We find what is quietly breaking you and take it out first.

01 — READ THE BODY, NOT THE SCALE

We start with what your body is telling us

Sleep, energy, digestion, stress load, and where it matters, your bloodwork. Fasting insulin, thyroid markers, lipids and how you recover tell the real story. A scale weight tells you almost nothing.

02 — SUBTRACTION BEFORE ADDITION

We remove the broken inputs first

Fat loss, energy and most lifestyle conditions start by taking out what is dysregulating you, not piling on new rules. Most people are overfed and undernourished at the same time.

03 — BUILT FOR YOUR REAL LIFE

The protocol fits your actual schedule

Indian meals, real portions, training that fits the time you genuinely have. A plan you can only follow on a perfect week is a plan that has already failed.

04 — SYSTEMS THAT SURVIVE A BAD WEEK

The result has to outlast the programme

Motivation runs out. Systems do not. We build a small set of non negotiables that hold when work explodes and you are tired, with weekly check ins and one coach who knows your case.
